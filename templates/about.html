About ReviewGuard

Fighting Fake Reviews
with Intelligence

ReviewGuard is an AI-powered platform dedicated to helping consumers identify computer-generated fake reviews and distinguish them from authentic human-written ones.

What is Fake Review Detection?

Fake review detection is the process of using computational methods to identify reviews that were not written by genuine customers. These reviews are often generated by bots, AI tools, or paid services to artificially inflate or deflate ratings of products and services. ReviewGuard uses natural language processing (NLP) and pattern recognition to classify reviews as Computer Generated (CG) or Original Reviews (OR).

CG — Computer Generated

  • Generic, repetitive phrases
  • No personal pronouns (I, my, we)
  • Perfectly uniform sentence structure
  • Lacks specific product details
  • Overuse of superlatives
  • Unnatural enthusiasm
  • No mention of personal experience

OR — Original Review

  • Personal pronouns used naturally
  • Specific product details mentioned
  • Informal language / typos possible
  • References to time/delivery
  • Mixed sentiment (pros and cons)
  • Mentions personal context
  • Unique writing style

Why It Matters

Studies estimate that up to 30-40% of online reviews are fake. These deceptive reviews mislead consumers into purchasing low-quality products, damage honest businesses, and erode trust in e-commerce platforms. ReviewGuard was built to give consumers and researchers a tool to verify the authenticity of reviews before making purchasing decisions.

Technology Behind ReviewGuard

ReviewGuard is built using Django (Python) on the backend with a custom NLP detection engine. The detector analyzes over 7 linguistic features including personal pronoun usage, sentence length variance, phrase pattern matching, sentiment uniformity, and informal language markers. Each review receives a confidence score indicating how strongly the system believes the classification.
